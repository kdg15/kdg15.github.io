Scoresheet

A simple app for keeping score while you play your favourite game. Open Scoresheet instead of grabbing a pencil and sheet of paper. Create a new scoresheet by entering player names. After a round of play enter the score for each player. Scoresheet will sum the score totals for you.

Written entirely in Swift by me.

Scoresheet is designed to have a sketchy appearance. Hope you enjoy using it playing games with family and friends.
